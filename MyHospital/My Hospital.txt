<h2>About</h2>
<p>

Our business model was built on providing passionate, dependable, consistent, trustworthy and affordable care to individuals of all ages. Our mission is to be your trusted source in helping you, your loved ones and your family achieve the maximum health care maintenance levels possible and live independently in the comfort of your own home.<br><br>

As your preferred health care provider, we have the resources, people, systems and knowledge necessary to make sure our patients get as much or as little help they require to live safely and independently at home.<br><br>

Our caregivers provide a variety of services which not only make daily living easier, but in the case of illness or injury, can also minimize time spent in the hospital and allow you or your loved one to remain at home.<br><br>

You deserve the utmost comfort, stability, and the building of a trusting relationship through having the same caregiver who knows you the best visiting and attending to your needs. The final choice of caregiver(s) is always yours. If for any reason, at any time, you decide to change the caregiver, simply give us a call and we will be happy to provide you with another caregiver that is compatible with your needs.<br><br>

We pride ourselves in providing an extensive range of quality in-home services, care in the home for individuals of all ages that require that extra help or companionship to remain in their own home, home health care, high-tech nursing care, rehabilitation, post hospital care or a respite for the family that cares for an ailing family member.<br><br>
